Teaching

Teaching interests and instructional experience in hospitality, tourism, accounting, operations, and analytics.

My teaching emphasizes conceptual clarity, analytical reasoning, and practical application in hospitality and tourism management. I aim to help students connect theory, quantitative thinking, and managerial decision making in service-industry settings.

Recent instructional roles include HTM 241 Managerial Accounting in Service Industries, HTM 141 Financial Accounting for the Service Industries, HTM 341 Operations Control and Analysis in the Hospitality Industry, and HTM 181 Lodging Management.
